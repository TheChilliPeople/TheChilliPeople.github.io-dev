The Original Sichuan Chilli Oil

Our first product, The Original Sichuan chilli oil. Made by hand in Edinburgh, each bottle contains 250ml of our lightly spicy, flavoursome oil. Add a splash to spice up your cooking or use as a condiment or dipping sauce.

Ingredients (in order of weight): rapeseed oil, Sichuan chilli flakes, Sichuan peppercorns, garlic, black vinegar (water, glutinous rice, wheat bran, sugar, salt), fine sea salt, coriander seed, cinnamon stick, star anise, black cardamom.
