The Chilli People

Bumbling around his family kitchen in Edinburgh, in the depths of a pandemic, Peter was flicking through an old Chinese cookbook when he found a recipe. From the first batch he knew he was onto something. After a few refinements he had created an irresistible, ruby red, generously warm and lightly spicy chilli oil.

It wasn't long before we'd all tried it, eating it in every way we could dream up. We joked that we needed to take it to the masses, that we would revolutionise the UK's condiment shelves. From there The Chilli People was born.
