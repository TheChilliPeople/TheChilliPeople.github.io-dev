About us

In the midst of a pandemic, in a kitchen in Edinburgh, Pete found a dusty Asian cookbook in the back of his cupboard. He had a look through, found a recipe and few hours later after a couple of adjustments, he had made a bottle of ruby red, generously warm and lightly spicy chilli oil.

It wasn't long before the rest of us tried it and once we did, we didn't stop. We ate it every day for the next few weeks, in every way we could dream up. We joked that we needed to take it to the masses. That joke is still going and it is why you're reading this now.
